Certification

Mumbai scuba diving club having only one philosophy. Try it - Experience it - Join Community.
The main purpose of the programme is to promote scuba diving as the biggest community of scuba divers in mumbai. As a sports activity and importance of training and certification
Club certification programmes are for those who are willing to do scuba diving training and explore the local scuba diving places in maharashtra. Programme offers 6 Amazing things before u go to scuba dive in the ocean.

  • Working of scuba equipment
  • How to handle scuba gear underwater
  • Communicating underwater
  • Communicating underwater.
  • How to swim underwater
  • Club Scuba diving Certification( Lifetime membership )
  • Short Dive package cost Rs 3500/-Pp + 18%GST
  • Long Dive package cost Rs- 5000/-Pp+ 18%GST
  • Programme include Trainning Dive + Ocean Dive
  • No of Days- 2 Days programme
  • Final Underwater ocean dive time 15-20 mins for Short Dive package
  • Final Underwater ocean dive time 45 mins for long Dive package.

Programme doesn't include stay - Transportation- food to the nearby scuba diving destination which is choosen for ocean diving
