About Us

Mumbai scuba diving club is newly operated club run by Mr. Yogesh Masurkar who is a PADI scuba instructor. Who finished his Master diver training in Dive Goa. Became an Instructor in Phuket - Thailand. He is diving in ocean since 2005 near beaches of Konkan Maharashtra.his scuba diving center in Malvan of Maharashtra where he created a job to the local fisherman and also creating marine life and coral reef conservation awareness programmes through the NGO called Sagari jaivavividhata sanvardhan v jatan sanstha . His Goals are to form a community of scuba divers in mumbai.
