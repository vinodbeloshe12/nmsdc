Welcome

Mumbai scuba diving club is newly operated club run by Mr. Yogesh Masurkar who is a PADI scuba instructor. Who finished his Master diver training in Dive Goa. Became an Instructor in Phuket - Thailand. He is diving in ocean since 2005 near beaches of Konkan Maharashtra. He has already opened his scuba diving center in Malvan of Maharashtra where he created a job to the local fisherman and also creating marine life and coral reef conservation awareness programmes through the NGO called Sagari jaivavividhata sanvardhan v jatan sanstha . His Goals are to form a community of scuba divers in mumbai.

Mr. Yogesh wants to promote ocean marine life conservation and Preservation by giving them a basic training of scuba diving in mumbai and giving them opportunities of diving in ocean and exploring new world and create awareness by putting artificial coral reef modules in the ocean and development of maximum marine habitat near beaches of maharashtra india.
